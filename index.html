⚠️ Best Viewed on Brave Browser

NowShowing works best with Brave Browser due to its aggressive ad and tracker blocking, which significantly improves performance with third-party streams.

Brave offers:

  • Better playback stability across all streaming sources
  • Fewer pop-ups, redirects, and unwanted ads
  • Faster loading with enhanced privacy

Why This Matters

NowShowing uses third-party embedded players (VidSrc, SuperEmbed, 2Embed and others). These sources often inject ads, redirect users, and behave inconsistently across browsers without strong ad blocking.

Recommendation

Use Brave Browser with its built-in shields, or add uBlock Origin to Chrome/Firefox. If a source doesn't load, try the next one — green buttons indicate likely-available streams.

Having trouble with this stream?

Streams can be unreliable due to ad blockers, geo-restrictions, high traffic, or temporary outages. Here's what to try:

  • Switch sources: Click a different source button — green means likely available
  • Disable ad blocker temporarily and reload
  • Try a different browser — Brave is recommended
  • Check your connection for stability issues

We strive to provide multiple options for a seamless experience. Thank you for your patience!
