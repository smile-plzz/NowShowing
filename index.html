⚠️ Best Viewed on Brave Browser

NowShowing works best with Brave Browser due to its aggressive ad and tracker blocking, which significantly improves performance with third-party streaming sources.

While other browsers may work, Brave offers:

  • Better playback stability across streaming sources
  • Fewer pop-ups, redirects, and unwanted ads
  • Faster loading with enhanced privacy

Why This Matters

NowShowing pulls streams via third-party embeds like VidSrc, SuperEmbed, and 2Embed. These sources often inject ads, redirect users, and load extra scripts that slow down or break the experience.

Brave's built-in protections handle all of that automatically — no extensions needed.

Having trouble with this stream?

Video streams can sometimes be unreliable. Common causes:

  • Browser Ad Blockers: Aggressive blockers may interfere with the player.
  • Geo Restrictions: Some sources aren't available in your region.
  • Source Overload: High traffic can cause buffering or failure.
  • Temporary Issues: The source may be momentarily down.

What to do:

  • Try Other Sources: Click the source buttons below the player. Green = likely working.
  • Disable Ad Blocker temporarily and reload.
  • Try a Different Browser — Brave is recommended.
